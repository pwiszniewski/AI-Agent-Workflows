# Research Plan: The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

## Objective
To research and locate information regarding the book 'The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy', including its background, availability, and cultural significance.

## Methodology

### 1. Contextual Research (Wikipedia)
- Use Wikipedia to compile a summary of the book.
- Key points to investigate:
    - Author: Douglas Adams
    - Publication year
    - Historical context (origins as a radio series)
    - Plot overview and genre

### 2. Availability Research (Google)
- Perform targeted Google searches to find how to access the book.
- Investigate:
    - Major online retailers (e.g., Amazon, Barnes & Noble).
    - Digital formats (E-books, Kindle).
    - Audiobook availability (e.g., Audible).
    - Potential library access (e.g., WorldCat or local library catalogs).

### 3. Media Research (YouTube)
- Use YouTube to find supplementary material to enrich the research.
- Content to search for:
    - Critical reviews or video essays about the series.
    - Interviews with the author (if available).
    - Summary videos to gain an understanding of the narrative tone.

### 4. Synthesis
- Consolidate all findings from the above steps into a comprehensive research report.
