Research Plan: Finding 'The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy'

Goal: To locate and obtain a copy of 'The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy' by Douglas Adams.

Methodology:
1. Verification of Metadata:
   - Use Wikipedia to confirm details: Author (Douglas Adams), Original Publication (1979), Genre (Science Fiction).
   - Ensure the search for the specific title matches the correct book edition.

2. Identification of Commercial Sources:
   - Use Google to find major retailers selling physical copies (e.g., Amazon, Barnes & Noble, local bookstores).
   - Use Google to identify digital platforms for ebooks and audiobooks (e.g., Audible, Kindle, Apple Books, Kobo).

3. Identification of Public Access:
   - Use Google to search for library availability using tools like Worldcat or local library digital systems (e.g., Libby, OverDrive).

4. Synthesis and Reporting:
   - Summarize the methods identified to obtain the book, categorizing them into:
     - Physical Purchase (New/Used)
     - Digital Purchase (Ebook/Audiobook)
     - Borrowing (Public Libraries)
